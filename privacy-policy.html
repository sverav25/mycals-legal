Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MyCals collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use the MyCals mobile application.

By using MyCals, you agree to the collection and use of information described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

MyCals may collect the following types of information:

Account Information

When you create an account or sign in, we may collect information such as your email address and user ID.

Profile Information

You may provide profile details such as:

  • Display name
  • Date of birth
  • Gender
  • Height
  • Weight
  • Activity level
  • Nutrition goal
  • Target weight
  • Avatar/profile image

Nutrition and Health-Related Tracking Data

MyCals allows you to track personal nutrition and wellness information, including:

  • Meals
  • Ingredients
  • Calories
  • Protein, carbohydrates, and fats
  • Saved meals
  • Water intake
  • Weight logs
  • Fasting or streak-related progress
  • Nutrition and water goals

Image and Barcode Logging Data

If you use image meal logging, barcode scanning, or similar features, MyCals may process:

  • Food images you choose to capture or upload
  • Barcode values
  • Food names and nutrition results
  • Ingredient and portion estimates

Image meal logging may use external services to analyze food images and generate nutrition estimates.

Subscription Information

If you purchase MyCals Premium, subscription status and purchase information may be processed through RevenueCat and the Apple App Store.

We do not directly collect or store your full payment card details.

Device and Usage Information

We may collect limited technical information needed to operate and improve the app, such as:

  • App usage events
  • Device type
  • Error logs
  • App performance information
  • Notification preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Save and display your meals, goals, water logs, weight logs, and progress
  • Personalize calorie, macro, and water recommendations
  • Provide barcode, search, and image meal logging functionality
  • Manage saved meals and history
  • Provide Premium subscription access
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Send reminders or notifications if enabled
  • Respond to support requests
  • Detect and fix bugs or technical issues

3. Subscriptions and Payments

MyCals Premium subscriptions are processed through the Apple App Store and managed using RevenueCat.

RevenueCat helps us check whether your subscription is active and whether Premium features should be unlocked.

We do not receive or store your full credit card or payment details.

Subscription management, cancellation, and refunds are handled by Apple according to Apple’s App Store policies.

4. Third-Party Services

MyCals may use third-party services to operate core features. These may include:

Supabase

Used for authentication, database storage, user profiles, meals, goals, logs, and related app data.

RevenueCat

Used for subscription management, entitlement checking, purchase status, and restoring purchases.

Apple App Store

Used for processing iOS subscriptions and payments.

Nutrition and Food Data Services

Used to search foods, resolve nutrition information, scan barcodes, or estimate nutrition values.

Image Processing or AI Services

Used when you choose to use image meal logging. Images may be processed to estimate food items, ingredients, and nutrition values.

5. How We Store Your Information

Your information may be stored securely using cloud database and storage services.

We take reasonable steps to protect your data, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Data Retention

We keep your information for as long as needed to provide the app’s features, maintain your account, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and improve the service.

You may request deletion of your account or personal data by contacting us at:

Email: sverav2@gmail.com

7. Your Choices

You can:

  • Update certain profile information in the app
  • Change your nutrition, water, and weight goals
  • Delete or edit saved meals where supported
  • Manage notifications through app settings or your device settings
  • Manage subscriptions through your Apple ID settings
  • Request account or data deletion by contacting support

8. Notifications

If you enable notifications, MyCals may send reminders related to meals, water intake, weigh-ins, or custom reminders.

You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings or app settings where available.

9. Children’s Privacy

MyCals is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

10. Health and Nutrition Disclaimer

MyCals provides nutrition and wellness tracking tools for informational purposes only.

MyCals does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Nutrition values, calorie targets, macro goals, image recognition results, and food data may be estimates and may not always be accurate.

You should consult a qualified healthcare professional before making significant changes to your diet, health, or fitness routine.

11. International Users

Your information may be processed and stored in countries outside your country of residence. By using MyCals, you agree that your information may be transferred and processed in those locations.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

If we make changes, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this policy.
