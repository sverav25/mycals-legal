Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MyCals collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use the MyCals mobile application.

MyCals is operated by Sven Ravnjak. If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal data, you can contact us at:

Email: sverav2@gmail.com

By using MyCals, you acknowledge that we collect and use information as described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

MyCals may collect the following types of information:

Account Information

When you create an account or sign in, we may collect information such as your email address, authentication provider information, and user ID.

Profile Information

You may provide profile details such as:

  • Display name
  • Date of birth
  • Gender
  • Height
  • Weight
  • Activity level
  • Nutrition goal
  • Target weight
  • Avatar/profile image
  • Unit preferences, such as weight, height, and water units

Nutrition and Health-Related Tracking Data

MyCals allows you to track personal nutrition and wellness information, including:

  • Meals
  • Ingredients
  • Calories
  • Protein, carbohydrates, and fats
  • Saved meals
  • Water intake
  • Weight logs
  • Fasting or streak-related progress
  • Nutrition, macro, water, and weight goals

Image and Barcode Logging Data

If you use image meal logging, barcode scanning, or similar features, MyCals may process:

  • Food images you choose to capture or upload
  • Barcode values
  • Food names and nutrition results
  • Ingredient and portion estimates

When you use image meal logging, your food image and related request data may be sent to an external AI service, such as Google Gemini, to identify food items, estimate portions, and generate nutrition estimates.

Subscription Information

If you purchase MyCals Premium, subscription status, entitlement status, and purchase-related information may be processed through RevenueCat and the Apple App Store.

We do not directly collect or store your full payment card details.

Device, Usage, and Diagnostic Information

We may collect limited technical information needed to operate, secure, and improve the app, such as:

  • App usage events
  • Device type
  • Error logs
  • Crash or performance information
  • App launch and interaction information
  • Notification preferences

Support Information

If you contact us for support, we may collect the information you provide in your message, such as your email address, account details, and the content of your support request.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Save and display your meals, goals, water logs, weight logs, and progress
  • Personalize calorie, macro, and water recommendations
  • Provide barcode, search, and image meal logging functionality
  • Manage saved meals and history
  • Provide MyCals Premium subscription access
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Send reminders or notifications if enabled
  • Respond to support requests
  • Detect, prevent, and fix bugs, abuse, or technical issues
  • Comply with legal, tax, accounting, and regulatory obligations

3. Legal Basis for Processing

If you are located in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or a region with similar data protection laws, we process your personal information based on one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract: to provide the app, account, tracking, subscription, and core features.
  • Consent: for optional features such as notifications, camera/photo access, and certain personalization features.
  • Legitimate interests: to improve the app, maintain security, prevent abuse, and fix technical issues.
  • Legal obligations: to comply with tax, accounting, payment, and legal requirements.

4. Subscriptions and Payments

MyCals Premium subscriptions are processed through the Apple App Store and managed using RevenueCat.

RevenueCat helps us check whether your subscription is active and whether Premium features should be unlocked.

We do not receive or store your full credit card or payment details.

Subscription management, cancellation, and refunds are handled by Apple according to Apple’s App Store policies.

5. Third-Party Services

MyCals may use third-party services to operate core features. These may include:

Supabase

Used for authentication, database storage, user profiles, meals, goals, logs, image storage, and related app data.

RevenueCat

Used for subscription management, entitlement checking, purchase status, paywall display, and restoring purchases.

Apple App Store

Used for processing iOS subscriptions, payments, subscription management, and refunds.

Nutrition and Food Data Services

Used to search foods, resolve nutrition information, scan barcodes, or estimate nutrition values.

Google Gemini or Other Image Processing / AI Services

Used when you choose to use image meal logging. Images and related request data may be processed to estimate food items, ingredients, portions, and nutrition values.

These third-party services may process information according to their own privacy policies and terms.

6. Tracking and Advertising

MyCals does not sell your personal information. MyCals does not use your personal information for third-party advertising tracking.

MyCals does not display third-party advertisements.

7. How We Store Your Information

Your information may be stored securely using cloud database, storage, authentication, and subscription management services.

We take reasonable steps to protect your data, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

We keep your information for as long as needed to provide the app’s features, maintain your account, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent fraud or abuse, and improve the service.

You may request deletion of your account or personal data by contacting us at:

Email: sverav2@gmail.com

  • Change your nutrition, water, weight, and unit preferences
  • Delete or edit meals and saved meals where supported
  • Manage notifications through app settings or your device settings

Depending on your location, you may have the right to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, or portability of your personal data. You may also withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise these rights, contact us at:

If you enable notifications, MyCals may send reminders related to meals, water intake, weigh-ins, end-of-day progress, or custom reminders.

You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings or app settings where available.

MyCals is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

MyCals does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Nutrition values, calorie targets, macro goals, image recognition results, barcode results, and food data may be estimates and may not always be accurate.

You should consult a qualified healthcare professional before making significant changes to your diet, health, or fitness routine.
